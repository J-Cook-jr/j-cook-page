Jeff Cook

Who Is Jeff Cook

Jeff Cook is a 28 year old Atlanta native and soon to be first generation web developer. He enjoys spending his free time outdoors in nature and he is intrigued by the untouched parts of the earth. His sparked interest in tech came from witnessing the way we depend upon it in our everyday lives. Being graced with a pioneering spirit, Jeff could not ignore his desire to become one of the greatest creators in the technology industry. He discovered his love for front end web development while working on his pre-work project for the March 2020 Digital Crafts immersive program in Atlanta, Ga. Front end web development has allowed him to tap in to his problem solving abilities in a creative way. As a person who enjoys hobbies such as writing poetry, music producing, painting and visual arts, learning to create web pages using HTML,CSS and Javascript was a smooth transition into the programming world for Jeff. He is a firm believer in people being able to do what they love and make a living while doing it. It will come as no surprise when Jeff enters the workforce as a highly motivated front end web developer after his successful completion of the full-stack web development course at Digital-crafts.

The Westside

A major part of what keeps Jeff focused on achieving greatness is the place he comes from, Atlanta’s westside neighborhood Grove Park. Grove Park is a historical neighborhood in Atlanta that was once home to many working class families. During the late 80’s and early 90’s it slowly started transforming into what is now considered one of the worst parts of Atlanta to reside in. The westside has become notorious for crimes such as illegal drug activity and gun violence. Jeff spent much of his childhood and teenage years in this environment, by the grace of God and with the guidance of his praying family he made it out of what some would consider an active war zone. Even though he was surrounded by poverty, his head was always on doing better for himself and his family. Jeff learned how to save money and financially provide for himself at the age of 15 when he got his first job working at Mc Donalds as a fry cook. He used his earnings to alleviate some of the financial strains of his single mother, whom was responsible for raising him and his younger brother. Even tho his early life was challenging, Jeff appreciates the struggle he and his family endured. He believes struggle promotes growth, it’s an integral part of what drives him everyday to be the best version of himself.

Achievements

Jeff has achieved much to be proud of. Just to list a few of his greatest achivements:

Georgia Goodwill Ambassador Award (Honored by Georgia State Govenor Brian P. Kemp)

U.S. Navy Veteran (Jeff was Honorably Discharged after serving four years of Active Duty in the U.S. Navy. He completed one successful deployment through the West Pacific Ocean. As a Quartermaster on board USS San Diego Jeff’s job was to safely navigate the ships crew during travel between foreign and domestic ports .)

Associates Degree in Music Business & Technology (After coming home from serving his country, Jeff attended the Sound & Audio Engineering institute of Atlanta and graduated with a 3.65 gpa.) This made him the first person in his immediate family to graduate college.

Interests & Hobbies

Jeff is a huge music fan: His favorite genres are - Hip Hop, Reggae, Jazz,Dubstep, and whatever genre Bassnectar is in.

He loves to travel - Jeff has been to Abu Dhabi, Hong Kong, Hawaii, Jordan and many cities in the United States.

Spending time with his family is also another one of Jeff’s favorite activities. He enjoys painting, watching movies, going to art and science museums, the Atlanta Zoo and the Georgia aquarium with his wife and daughter.

When he has time he goes to his “Lab” and works on his music production craft.

He enjoys working out and biking for hours on one of Atlanta’s hidden jewels, the silver comet trail. He also enjoys spending his off days on Stone Mountain park's golf course.

Family

Jeff loves his family, he is married and has one 4 year old daughter. On his fathers side of the family he is the second oldest of ten siblings. He has one younger brother on his mothers side and plenty of cousins both sides of his family tree, some of whom he has yet to meet. His ancestors are of West African descent, many of them were from Benin, Togo and Congo Africa.
